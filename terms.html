Safety & Compliance Checklist

For the Instructor (The Driver)
  • License Verification: Ensure you have a valid, unexpired US Driver’s License. In New York, you must be at least 21 years old to supervise a learner permit holder.
  • Insurance Confirmation: Verify that your personal auto insurance policy does not exclude “for‑hire” instruction.
  • Vehicle Integrity: Perform a “walk‑around” before the student arrives. Check brake lights, turn signals, and headlights.
  • Clean Record: You represent that you have no major traffic violations (DUI, reckless driving) within the last 3 years.
  • Identity Sync: Confirm the Student’s name and photo in the app before they enter the vehicle.
For the Student (The Learner)
  • Permit Check: You must have your physical Learner’s Permit on your person.
  • Safety Contact: Share your live location or lesson schedule with a friend or family member.
  • Vehicle Comfort: Wear closed‑toe, flat shoes.
  • Visual Check: Confirm the Instructor’s license plate and face match the profile shown in the app.
The Vehicle Standard
  • Registration: Must have a valid, current registration sticker and inspection.
  • Tires: Adequate tread depth and proper inflation.
  • Interior: Clear of loose debris that could roll under pedals.
  • Mirrors: Side and rearview mirrors must be present and adjustable.
New York State “Supervision” Rules
  • Immediate Supervision: Instructor must sit in the front passenger seat and be alert at all times.
  • Restricted Areas: Avoid no‑permit zones unless authorized.
  • Passenger Limits: For students under 18, follow passenger restrictions.
