Terms of Service: drivingLynX

  1. Nature of the Service & Provider Tiers
    drivingLynX is a technology marketplace connecting individuals seeking driving practice or test transportation (“Students”) with independent service providers (“Providers”). drivingLynX is not a driving school, transportation network company, or employer. We facilitate connections across two distinct tiers:
    • Certified Mentors: Licensed driving instructors offering formal instructional services (with or without a vehicle).
    • Transit Drivers: Fully licensed drivers offering a "Take me to driving test" service (transporting the Student to the test location, waiting, and returning). Transit Drivers are strictly prohibited from offering driving instruction.
  2. Strict Onboarding & Certification Requirements
    To utilize the platform and set up payment processing, Providers must meet strict criteria:
    • Mentors: Must digitally certify and provide proof of active affiliation with a state-licensed driving school, possess valid instructor credentials, and attest to having a minimum of 1,000 hours of instructional experience.
    • Transit Drivers: Must hold a valid, unrestricted US Driver's License and comply with all state-specific livery, rideshare, or "for-hire" transportation regulations.
  3. Insurance Disclaimers & "For-Hire" Exclusions
    Important: drivingLynX provides absolutely no insurance coverage.
    Personal auto insurance policies typically contain a "livery exclusion," meaning coverage is voided if the vehicle is used to transport passengers for a fee.
    • Provider Responsibility: Mentors and Transit Drivers are solely responsible for maintaining commercial auto insurance, driving school insurance, or Transportation Network Company (TNC) endorsements that legally cover "for-hire" activities and third-party instruction.
    • Student Responsibility: Students agree they enter vehicles at their own risk and waive all right to claim damages against drivingLynX for auto accidents, injuries, or property damage.
  4. Payments, Escrow, and Booking Fees
    drivingLynX acts as a limited payment collection agent.
    • Rates: Providers set their own hourly rates for Instructional Services or Test Transit Services.
    • Booking Fee: drivingLynX applies a platform booking fee to each transaction, disclosed to the Student at checkout, to maintain the technology infrastructure.
    • Escrow: Funds are collected from the Student at the time of booking and held in escrow. Funds are disbursed to the Provider only after the service is marked as successfully completed by both parties.
  5. Limitation of Liability & Indemnification
    To the maximum extent permitted by law, drivingLynX shall not be liable for any direct, indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. Providers and Students agree to indemnify and hold drivingLynX harmless from any claims, legal penalties, traffic violations, or accidents arising from the use of the platform. We do not guarantee the skill, safety, or legal compliance of any user.

Safety & Compliance Checklist

For the Provider (Mentors & Transit Drivers)
  • Commercial Compliance: Ensure your insurance explicitly covers commercial "for-hire" transportation or private instruction.
  • Vehicle Integrity: Perform a walk‑around before the Student arrives. Ensure registration and inspections are fully up to date.
  • Identity Sync: Confirm the Student’s name and photo in the app before they enter the vehicle.
  • Scope of Service: Transit Drivers must not provide driving instruction, tips, or guidance. Their role is strictly point-A to point-B transportation.
For the Student (The Learner)
  • Permit Check: You must have your physical Learner’s Permit on your person at all times.
  • Safety Contact: Share your live location and Provider details with a friend or family member before entering the vehicle.
  • Visual Check: Confirm the Provider’s license plate, vehicle make/model, and face match the profile shown in the drivingLynX app.
New York State & Local Jurisdiction Rules
  • Supervision: For instructional rides, the Mentor must sit in the front passenger seat and be alert at all times.
  • Regulatory Adherence: Providers are solely responsible for adhering to all local Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) and Taxi & Limousine Commission (TLC) or equivalent regulations in their operating area.
